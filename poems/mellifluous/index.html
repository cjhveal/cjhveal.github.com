mellifluous

Written 2 years ago on May 8th, 2013

a manly muscled muffin,
mumbling macabbe machavelian manifestos a mile a miniute,
is mellowed as melic melodies multiply the moment,
mirthfully immersed in milk and merciful manna,
and a million miniscule malachite machinations march madly,
making a myriad of multilayered mandelic markings
in mauve and magenta.
